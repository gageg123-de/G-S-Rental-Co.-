FAQ

Common questions before you book

What ages is soft play best for?

Soft play is best for babies, toddlers, and young children, typically ages 6 months to 5 years. Adult supervision is required at all times.

Do you sanitize equipment?

Yes. Equipment is cleaned, sanitized, and inspected before each rental so every setup arrives event-ready.

Can setups be outdoors?

Yes, outdoor setups may be available depending on weather, surface type, shade, and venue access. Covered areas are preferred.

How much space is needed?

Space needs vary by package. Smaller setups can fit many living rooms, while premium setups need a larger open area.

How far in advance should I book?

Booking at least 3 to 6 weeks ahead is recommended, especially for weekends and popular party seasons.
